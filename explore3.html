Mobile App Development Course

Become proficient in mobile app development with our 6-month comprehensive course. Learn to build apps for Android and iOS using modern frameworks, and gain hands-on experience in app design, architecture, and deployment.

What You Will Learn

1. Fundamentals of Mobile App Development

Understand the basics of mobile app development, including platform differences, mobile architecture, and UI/UX design principles.

2. Android Development

Develop Android apps using Java and Kotlin. Work with Android Studio and get hands-on experience building responsive and optimized apps.

3. iOS Development

Learn to develop iOS applications using Swift and Xcode. Build elegant apps tailored for iOS devices with a focus on performance and design.

4. Cross-Platform Development

Explore frameworks like React Native and Flutter to build apps for both Android and iOS from a single codebase. Understand best practices for cross-platform development.

5. App Deployment & Maintenance

Learn to deploy apps to Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Maintain and update apps, and understand app monetization strategies.

Key Features of the Course

  • 6-month duration with flexible learning
  • Hands-on projects and real-world app building
  • Guidance from industry experts
  • Job-oriented training with placement support
